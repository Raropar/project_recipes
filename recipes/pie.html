Nellie & Joe's Key Lime Pie

Description:

A tart and refreshing pie made with the distinctive taste of Key West limes. Perfect for a summer night dessert.

Ingredients:

  • 9 inch graham cracker pie crust
  • 14 ounce can of sweetened condensed milk
  • 3 egg yolks (whites not used)
  • 1/2 cup Nellie & Joeâ€™s Key West Lime Juice

Steps:

  1. Combine milk, egg yolks and lime juice.
  2. Blend until smooth.
  3. Pour filling into pie crust and bake at 350 degrees F for 15 minutes.
  4. Allow to stand 10 minutes before refrigerating.
  5. Just before serving, top with freshly whipped cream, or meringue, and garnish with lime slices.
