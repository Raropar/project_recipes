Tricolor Rotini Pasta Salad

Description:

A tri-colored Italian pasta, flavored plain, spinach, and tomato, with whatever vegetables and add-ins you like; the sky's the limit!

Ingredients:

  • 1 box tricolor rotini
  • 1 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons Italian Dressing
  • 3 plum tomatoes, diced
  • 1 8-oz package fresh mozzarella, drained
  • 1/2 cup diced cucumber
  • 1/2 cup edamame
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Bring a large pot of water to a boil and cook the pasta al dente.
  2. Toss the pasta with olive oil and place on a sheet tray to cool down.
  3. Once the pasta is cool, combine all the ingredients in a large bowl and gently toss to combine.
