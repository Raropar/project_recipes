Spicy Miso Ramen

Let's make a delicious bowl of spicy miso ramen noodles.

This easy and quick homemade spicy miso ramen noodle recipe gives the timeless Japanese dish an extra kick. It features add-ons such as jammy soy-sauce eggs, boiled bok choy, and rich pork belly slices, topped with chili flakes and sesame seeds.

Ingredients:

  • Ramen noodles
  • Chicken or pork stock

Steps:

  1. Bring a pot of water to a boil, add salt, add noodles. When noodles soft, remove from heat.
